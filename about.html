GET TO KNOW ME

Passion for Design: From Startup to corporations, My journey so far

Hi, I’m Fathima Shamma, a passionate freelance UI/UX designer and developer based in Calicut. With a strong foundation in software development, I bridge the gap between functionality and aesthetics to create seamless, user-centered digital experiences.

My journey in tech began with coding, which gave me a deep understanding of how things work behind the scenes. This technical expertise allows me to design not just beautiful interfaces, but also practical and scalable solutions. Over the years, I’ve honed my skills in user research, wireframing, prototyping, and responsive design, ensuring that every project I work on is both visually appealing and highly functional.

Beyond design, I’m also a content creator, sharing insights, tips, and tutorials to help others grow in the UI/UX and web development space. I’m deeply passionate about empowering individuals and businesses to achieve their goals through thoughtful design and innovative solutions. Let’s collaborate and bring your ideas to life!

My Process: From
Concept to Launch

01

Discovery & Research

Understanding your vision, goals, and target audience through research and brainstorming.

02

Wireframing & UI Design

Sketching out simple, grayscale wireframes to focus on structure and functionality rather than aesthetics. This allows for quick revisions and better planning.

03

Design Turning Ideas into Visuals

This is where creativity meets strategy. I design a user-friendly interface that is not only visually appealing but also enhances usability and supports your brand’s goals.

04

Development

Using Webflow, I translate the design into a fully functional, interactive website. This includes animations, responsive layouts for all devices, and SEO optimization for better visibility.

05

Launch

Once the website is ready, I handle deployment and ensure a smooth handover. I provide training so you can manage updates easily, along with options for ongoing support and maintenance.
